Welcome to Twyst!

The Twyst service helps you earn rewards at the places you visit frequently, and find new places that you may like to eat out at. We built the Twyst service to solve some of the problems that we observed & faced. And so, Twyst was born - as they say, you're the best person to scratch your itch!

"I don't like Zomato! The reviews & ratings are undifferentiated, and not at all based on people like me. What if I could be told of places to eat out at, based on where I've eaten out in the past, and where my friends are eating out at? That would be so cool!"

Rahul

"See, all these restaurants - especially the small ones. They are spending on ads in the paper, building iOS apps, spending money on listing services - and they don't have any idea if it is working at all! Also, they have no way to track who their customers are, and no way to see who's coming back often. What if every restaurant - small or big - had a way to accurately know who thier customers are, and reward the ones who came back often? It would just change how customer engagement is done!

Manyu

"I frequently visit this coffee shop - maybe 5 times a week - but the staff doesn't know me or even recognize me. Just to hear someone say 'Welcome back, sir' would make such a difference. And if they gave me a free coffee for coming back, I wouldn't say No to that!"

Arun
