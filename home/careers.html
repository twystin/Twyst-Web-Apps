Frontend Developer HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript

We are looking for a front-end engineer who can join us and build out a beautiful & engaging user experience for our web site, our merchant product & the phone application.

  • Responsibilities
    • Collaborate within our team to build beautiful and and engaging user experiences
    • Plan, build and launch features.
    • Participate in software design, architecture, and research.
    • Continually broaden and refine your own skills and knowledge
    • Collaborate with our backend engineer
    • Iterate, Iterate, Iterate
  • Skills & Requirements
    • 1+ year of experience working on interactive web applications.
    • Great coding skills that are showcased in the quality products that you have shipped
    • Extensive knowledge of HTML5, CSS3, Javascript, REST, JSON, and in-depth experience with a frontend responsive framework (such as Bootstrap or Foundation)
    • Strong knowledge of version control systems (Git preferred; we use GitHub)
    • Experience with responsive design implementation
    • Experience with working in an agile development environment
    • Experience with AngularJS and NodeJS is a bonus!
