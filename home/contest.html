Terms & Conditions

1. The contest is open to users with the Twyst app installed on their iPhone or Android phones ONLY. The contest is not open to owners and employees of outlets participating in the contest.

2. The contest will run for a period of 6 weeks, starting on 08-Sep 2014 and ending on 19-Oct 2014.

3. User registration for the contest is mandatory. In case a user fails to complete the registration form, his/her Twyst activity (check-ins and voucher redemptions) will not be considered for the contest.

4. User check-ins and voucher redemptions done only via the Twyst app for iPhone or Android will be eligible for the contest. Check-ins by users done by filling out visit cards at Twyst outlets, and voucher redemptions completed by showing voucher SMS messages to outlet staff will not be eligible for the contest.

5. User check-ins and redemptions via the app only at the ‘participating outlets’ will be considered for the scoring each week. The list of participating outlets for each week will be available under the ‘Outlets You Can Twyst At’ section.

6. There will be 3 categories be one winner per week in each category. If there are more than one person at the same ‘score’ (no. of check-ins / redemptions / outlets), the person who reaches that ‘score’ first during that week will be adjudged the winner.

7. Weekly vouchers will be for use at HUDA Metro Park outlets that are on Twyst only.

8. The grand prize will be awarded to the user with the highest number of check-ins over the duration of the contest. The grand prize vouchers will be eligible for use at any Twyst outlet.

9. Fraudulent check-ins and voucher redemptions will lead to the user being disqualified from the contest. Twyst.in’s decisions in this regard will be final.
