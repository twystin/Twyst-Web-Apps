ELIGIBILITY:

If you are an individual legal resident of the Republic of India and are 18 years or above at the time of entry, you are eligible to enter the Contest.

HOW TO ENTER:

You may enter the Contest beginning at 00:00:01 a.m. (IST) on 8th September, 2014 and ending at 11:59:59 p.m. (IST) on 19th October, 2014 (the "Contest Period"). To enter, you must:

(1) Download the Twyst App on your phone (The Twyst App is available for iPhone and Android phones only) and sign in during the Contest Period

(2) Sign up for the contest at http://twyst.in/metropark Signing up is mandatory; in case you do not complete the registration form, your Twyst activity (Check-ins and Voucher Redemptions) will not be considered for the contest

(3) Check-in and/or redeem rewards at any of the participating outlets using the Twyst Mobile Phone App

HOW TO PARTICIPATE:

(1) The Contest has three categories, for each of which one winner will be announced weekly:

a. ‘Most Check-ins’ – The contestant with the highest number of Check-ins in the week will be awarded the prize in this category.

b. ‘Most Redemptions’ – The contestant with the highest number of Voucher Redemptions in the week will be awarded the prize in this category.

c. ‘Check-ins at most outlets’ – The contestant who has checked-in to the maximum number of Participating Outlets in the week will be awarded the prize in this category.

(2) Only check-ins at outlets participating in this Contest will be considered for the purpose of this contest. For a complete list of all the Participating Outlets and their corresponding periods of participation please see section below.

(3) Only Check-ins completed by scanning a Twyst QR code at a Participating Outlet through the Twyst Mobile App, or voucher redemptions performed using the Twyst App will be considered for the purpose of this contest. Check-ins done by filling out Visit Cards at Participating Outlets, and voucher redemptions completed by showing voucher SMS messages to outlet staff will not be eligible for the contest.

(4) Tie Break: In case of a tie between contestants, the criteria used to decide the prize will be awarded to the person who achieves the winning ‘score’ earlier in the week.

(5) Each Monday-Sunday period beginning 08-Sep till 19-Oct will be considered a ‘week’ in this contest.
