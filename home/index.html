How to Twyst

Discover

Find exclusive #TwystRewards at the top restaurants, cafes and pubs around town. New rewards getting added every week to help you get more out eating out!

Check-in

Check-in whenever you visit a #TwystRewards location. It is easy – scan a QR code with the app, or just fill out a visit card when you are there. You can check-in when you order in as well!

Unlock #TwystRewards

..And when you unlock a #TwystReward, we will send the vouchers right to your phone (if you don't have the app, you will get the voucher by SMS). Using the reward is easy - just show the server the voucher when you visit or order next!

Twyst is available in Delhi NCR at present.
