Twyst is a technology start-up based out of Gurgaon, NCR. Twyst allows retailers to create powerful and yet flexible marketing programs to improve customer loyalty. The Twyst phone app gives users personalized and relevant recommendations to outlets, and allows them to get recognized and rewarded at the places they frequent and love. We are fun, casual and passionate about what we do, and we’re looking to add more people to the team who are similar to us! Some benefits of working at Twyst are:

  • Friendly, fun and casual co-workers.
  • Small, and very smart team!
  • Professional development, mentoring & growth
  • In-house cricket league!

The Twyst Team

Abhimanyu Lal

A graduate of IIM Ahmedabad, Abhimanyu is passionate about Marketplaces and their role in providing market access to ‘small’ establishments.

Rahul Chakraborti
