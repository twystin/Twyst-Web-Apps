The Twyst FAQs

Why should I use Twyst?

Wish you got a little bit more for frequenting your favourite joints? Want to get more out of eating out? Then Twyst is for you.

On Twyst, you can now earn rewards at some of the coolest restaurants, cafes and pubs in town based on how often you visit, special invites and privileges for being a loyal customer, and overall getting the star treatment that you deserve!

What, still wondering?

I don't have an iPhone or an Android phone. Does that mean no Twyst rewards for me?

Of course not! You can fill out a visit card to check-in at outlets that are running reward programs on Twyst. You will receive your reward notifications by SMS. Just ask the outlet staff to help!

It works better with the app of course, you can discover more locations and rewards, check-in and track rewards, receive timely notifications and stay connected much better.

Which locations have Twyst rewards?

We have a select set of locations in Gurgaon (and a few in Delhi) on Twyst right now, and are adding more all the time. Get the app to find out where Twyst rewards are available.
